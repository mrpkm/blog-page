Top 5 Affiliate Programs 2022

Affiliate Marketing is the branch of Digital Marketing. It is a type of commission based marketing in which a company gives rewards or commission to one or more affiliates(third party) for providing visitorsor customers who will then purchase the products of that company by going through the links generated by the affiliates.

Affiliate Marketing can be started through many approaches, like writing blog posts, creating large audiences through Youtube,Instagram,Facebook and many other social media platforms. There are many affiliate programs that provide affiliate links of different products according to your niche.

Amazon Associates

Amazon AssociatesAmazon Associates is an Affiliate Marketing program that allows content Creators, website owners and bloggers to create links and earn referral income when customers click the links and Buy products from Amazon through that links. This affilate program is very good and convenient for the affiliate marketing beginners as it is completely free to join and easy to use.

As Amazon is one of the largest and Most popular retailers in the world, consumers are conveniently buy the products Through the referal links that take them directly to the main page of the amazon e-store. The commission on amazon associates varies from 0.2% to 10% as per the product description.

There is some problem with Amazon Affiliate that it provides less commissions as compared with the other affiliate programs.secondly, Amazon remembers the traffic you sent to their site if they add something to their cart. Until they pay for the products, you will be remembered for 90 days, but if people will visit and won't buy instantly, amazon will remember the cookie for 24 hours long.

ClickBank Affiliate program

ClickBank ClickBank is a global e-commerce and affiliate site. It was founded in 1998. It is a platform for physical and digital products,digital content creators and affiliate marketers. The digital products includes, e-books,sotware,and videos.It is regarded as the best place for the people to buy or sell their digital products. The publishers of the products provide commission to the affiliates who promote their products in affiliate's website, blogs, and many social media platforms.

Whenever publishers launch their new products, they offers a certain percentage of their product's value as commission to affiliates. Affiliates can earn commissions upto 75%. There are no registration fees and no hidden charges to becoming an affilate.

In the ClickBank marketplace, sellers and affiliates cannot communicate directly. They must go via their own respective websites. It contains so many products that it can be difficult for the affiliates to choose best from the among. As this affiliate program is running for a very long period, there are lots of competition in this market.

Shopify affiliate Program

Shopify Affiliate Program The Shopify Affiliate Program is a digital platform that provides support to educators, review sites, influencers and content creators to educate and inspire their audience about enterpreneurship with shopify and earn commission for their referrals.

Shopify provides a high commission rate to their affiliates. For each referral who sign up for a paid plan, you will receive a 100 percent commission on the first and second payment. It also provides the 200n percent commission that applies to all plans, excluding shopify plus.This program has a 30-days cookie. That means when a person clicks on your link and sign up for a free trial within 30 days and if the user buy the paid version, you will earn a commission.

This affiliate program is also very good platform but it will be quite inconvenient for someone unfamiliar with deep linking as it required to deep-link their IDs to page and post URLs.

Fiverr Affiliate Program

this Fiverr Affilate ProgramFiverr is an online marketplace that provides digital services where individuals or companies can find freelancers with a skill that can fulfill companie's or individual's need. It contains a wide range of services provided by the professionals.

Fiverr Affiliate program allows you to earn commission by referring products and services provided by the freelancers. This program has a dynamic commission structure. You can earn the commission only if someone makes a purchase through your affiliate link. The commission rate depends on the type of the service that the buyer purchases. The best thing about this program is the cookie duration. If a new customer clicks on one of your affliate links, you will get commission for the duration of a whole year.

There is one disadvantage with the program is that , if you refer Fiverr services or products to people who already have a Fiverr account, you aren't going to get any commission from your referral link, even if that person never purchased the products or services through that account. Otherwise, this affiliate program is very helpful for those who want to start their journey of affiliate marketing.

CJ Affiliate

CJ AffiliateCJ Affiliate ( Commission Junction) is the world's largest and most trusted online affiliate program owned by Publicis Groupe. This global affiliate program is a great marketplace for the blog and website owners to start the affiliate marketing program offered by thousands of online brands.

Affiliates use CJ Affiliate to find products to promote and earn commission for referrals on those products. Affiliates also use PPC compaigns, SEO campaigns, RSS, and emails, as well as a number of other means.

The CJ Affiliate’s affiliate program features three types of payment models. You can choose between a cost per acquisition (CPA), pay-per-sale (PPC), pay-per-lead (PPC), and pay-per-view (PPC). CJ also offers flat-spend opportunities. Each of these models can be profitable to you, depending on your level of expertise. But there are some things you should know before signing up. While you can customize your code to fit your website, it is vital to note that you should update it regularly so that your website remains current and relevant. Some CJ affiliate codes include additional parameters like click domains and additional affiliate codes. Regardless of which type of link you choose, it is essential to remember that CJ Affiliate’s affiliate program is highly targeted and requires targeted traffic to make it successful. But there is one problem with this program that the site is bulky and not very user-friendly for beginners.
